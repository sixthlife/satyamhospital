Privacy Policy

Satyam Hospital is dedicated to providing a comprehensive range of medical services to meet the diverse healthcare needs of our patients. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information Collection

We may collect personal information, such as your name, contact information, date of birth, and medical history, to provide you with the best healthcare services. We may collect this information through our website, in-person interactions, or other communication channels.

2. Data Usage

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Provide medical services and treatment.
  • Contact you regarding appointments and health-related matters.
  • Improve our website and services.
  • Comply with legal requirements.

3. Data Security

We take appropriate measures to secure your data and protect it from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Your information is stored securely and accessible only to authorized personnel. We maintain strict confidentiality and adhere to industry-standard security practices.

4. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. You can modify your browser settings to control cookie usage, but please note that some features of our website may be affected.

5. Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as necessary to reflect changes in our services or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on our website, and we encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@satyamhospital.com.
